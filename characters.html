Meet the Ohana

Stitch (Experiment 626)

A blue alien genetic experiment created by Dr. Jumba Jookiba. Originally designed to cause chaos and destruction, Stitch learns the true meaning of family through his bond with Lilo. He's incredibly strong, nearly indestructible, and has advanced learning capabilities.

Voiced by: Chris Sanders

Species: Genetic Experiment

Special Abilities: Super strength, intelligence, four arms

Experiment Ohana Hero

Lilo Pelekai

A spirited 6-year-old Hawaiian girl who adopts Stitch as her "dog." She loves Elvis Presley music, practices hula dancing, and deeply believes in 'ohana - that family means nobody gets left behind or forgotten. Her kindness transforms Stitch from a destructive force into a loving family member.

Voiced by: Daveigh Chase

Age: 6 years old

Interests: Hula dancing, Elvis music, photography

Human Hawaiian Kind Heart

Nani Pelekai

Lilo's 19-year-old sister and legal guardian who struggles to care for Lilo after their parents died in a car crash. She works hard to keep their family together while dealing with the challenges of sudden responsibility.

Voiced by: Tia Carrere

Guardian Sister

Dr. Jumba Jookiba

The Kweltikwan mad scientist who created Stitch (Experiment 626) through illegal genetic experimentation. Initially antagonistic, he becomes part of Lilo's extended ohana.

Voiced by: David Ogden Stiers

Scientist Alien

Agent Pleakley

A one-eyed Plorgonarian Galactic Federation agent who serves as Earth's expert. Often neurotic and overly cautious, he accompanies Jumba on his mission to capture Stitch.

Voiced by: Kevin McDonald

Agent Expert
